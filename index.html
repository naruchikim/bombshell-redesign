About.


Bombshell is the exclusive domain of the stylish and confident woman seeking plus size clothing to match her confidence and beauty. We are the only boutique in the Twin Cities catering to this overlooked and under-served market of women who wear sizes 14-24. Our claim to fame is our selection of unique plus size designer lines (such as Elena Mirò, Lafayette 148 New York and Cabiria) that are not found anywhere else in the area, and often, no where else in the country!
We believe that beauty is open to everyone. We believe the definition of beauty needs to be expanded and diversified in this world. And we aim to accomplish our mission one gorgeously put-together woman at a time!

Persona.


From the illustrious house of MaxMara comes Persona by Marina Rinaldi, dedicated to women who don’t want to give up quality clothes and a contemporary, feminine style simply because they wear plus-sizes (sounds like our girl!). Persona by Marina Rinaldi has a dynamic and strong personality paired with classic sensibility. Not only we will debut this line at Bombshell in fall of 2015, we will also have part of the couture collection they designed specifically for Berlin Curvy Plus fashion week!
